# Vision

Landfall-forge teaches agents how to arrive inside a repo, refresh reality, and turn new evidence into the right local operating state before acting.

A landfall is a named, purpose-specific arrival ritual. It is not a generic summary. It declares which evidence surfaces to refresh, what questions to answer, what must be tasked out, what human-only actions to stop on, and where the results should be written in the repo.

## North Stars

- Every operational repo can define many landfalls, each with a clear job.
- Landfalls are written as repo-local contracts, not hidden habits.
- Fresh evidence changes tasks, payables, wiki pages, calendars, and next actions.
- Agents stop before credentials, legal judgment, payments, identity checks, and wire/bank details unless the human explicitly drives the step.

## Guardrails

- No software. This forge ships skills and templates only.
- Landfalls must be evidence-first, not vibes-first.
- Landfalls must be scoped. A payables landfall should not pretend to be a legal landfall.
- Landfalls must write durable results back into the repo or explicitly say why they did not.
